Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we protect it.

1. No Data Upload

iZeroPDF is a 100% client-side application. No files, images, or data are ever uploaded to any server. Everything you process stays on your device.

2. Local Storage Only

We use your browser's local storage to save:

  • Conversion History - Track files you've converted
  • Usage Statistics - Your visit count and file conversions
  • Theme Preference - Your light/dark mode choice
  • Cache Consent - Your preference for offline caching

All this data stays on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

3. No Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics. No third-party tracking scripts are loaded.

4. No Analytics

We do not collect or send any analytics data. Your usage remains completely private.

5. Open Source

iZeroPDF is fully open source. You can review the code yourself to verify our privacy claims.
